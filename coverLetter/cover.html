Career Statement

I am a Secondary Teacher in Cross and Passion College, Kilcullen with Art as my main subject along with Senior Cycle Computer Science I am currently participating in the phase 4 Leaving Certificate Computer Science programme run by the PDST and have just recently began teaching Computer Science to 5th Years with a view to my students completing the Leaving Cert Computer Science Examinations in 2024

My Qualifications include a BDES, HDIP in Art Education and a Certificate in Web Design and Development and I have been teaching Post primary level for over 14 years.I acurrently studying to obtain a HDIPComp in NCI, Dublin. I also have valuable experience working in the private sector in the areas of Web Design and Development including HTML programming and Web authoring, Multimedia production - Audio and Video, User Interface Design and Graphic design.

Throughout my Professional life I have had a keen interest in all aspects of computer technology - particularly web and multimedia technologies and audio production. I love both hardware and software and have tried to keep abreast of new and emerging technologies.

My background in both Art and Design and Web Design and Development has equipped me with many skills that are applicable to Computer Science and software development such as creative thinking, planning and design development, research, investigating and problem solving. Other hands on programming / technical skills I have experience with include HTML, CSS and Java Script, Python, MiroBit and Block based coding.
